I've worked in startups from seed to Series C stage, and in enterprise at Gannett (media) and Capital One (finance). I found my home in UX-centric product management on small, nimble teams.

Experience

Lead teams through user research, ideation, rapid prototyping, and building/shipping. I manage stakeholder relationships and periodically pitch our leadership team for continued funding.

Optimized existing survey channels to increase response rate by > 5%. Developed weekly reports to connect user feedback to deployment reports, support protocols, and Pivotal Tracker stories. Wrote HTML and CSS to help ship Omada's Underserved Populations program.

I managed the full product development cycle (market research and concepting through development and launch) and interdisciplinary team (engineering, product, design) over the course of 9 mos.

Wrote demand gen pieces targeting Sales Executives. Wrote sales enablement documents mapping product features to workflows of key user personas. Created competitive framework to evaluate new players in the enterprise business analytics space.
